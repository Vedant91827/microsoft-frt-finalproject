Your search for an appropriate destination in Kanpur to build your dream residence ends here. Property Specifications
North-east facing, this residential plot measures an area of 900 sqyrd and is in a gated community. Hence, residents here can be assured of living in a secure environment. Also, with several constructions done within the vicinity; residents here will get to experience the friendly atmosphere and meet more like-minded people, which is a plus point.
